Curating Sustainable Alternatives.

Thulira exists to curate sustainable alternatives for Indian households. We discovered eha's incredible biocomposite materials and decided to bring them directly to customers who care about the planet.

Our Promise

Durability

Our products are designed to last. Unlike clay or ceramic alternatives that easily crack or break, our biocomposite materials withstand everyday drops and impacts.

01

Non-Toxic & Safe

Tested for food safety. Our products are 100% free from BPA, melamine, formaldehyde, and PFAS, ensuring that your family's health is always protected.

02

Made in India & Transparent

Locally manufactured to support the local economy. We also provide full transparency into our materials—blending agricultural crop-waste with a small amount of polypropylene to bind it.

03

Thulira is an authorized reseller of eha (eha.eco) sustainable products.

Our Proven
Process

Sourcing Crop-Waste

We partner with local farmers to collect agricultural crop waste, giving a second life to materials that would otherwise be discarded or burned.

Innovative Biocomposite Blending

Our engineers blend the crop waste with a small amount of non-toxic polypropylene, creating a highly durable, food-safe biocomposite material.

Sustainable Manufacturing

We locally manufacture our drinkware and home products in energy-efficient facilities, ensuring a low carbon footprint and supporting the local economy.

Delivering To Your Home

Your new sustainable products are packaged in eco-friendly materials and shipped directly to you, ready to help you build a waste-free lifestyle.
